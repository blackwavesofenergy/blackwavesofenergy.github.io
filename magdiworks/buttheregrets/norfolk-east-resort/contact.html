Where to find us

Norfolk East Resort is located at 13 Marine Parade, Great Yarmouth, Norfolk. We're open 24/7, 365 days of the year.


The nearest train station is at Great Yarmouth Station, from there use the 2 or X1 buses or use an 8-minute long taxi fare.
